Item #: SCP-007

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures:

SCP-007 is to be contained in a standard secured living quarters at Site-19. The subject is permitted to move freely within the facility, provided it does not attempt to leave or engage in any behavior deemed hazardous by Foundation personnel.

  1. : Daily medical examinations are to be conducted to monitor the condition of SCP-007 and the anomaly within.

  2. : No invasive procedures are to be performed on SCP-007 without prior approval from at least two (2) Level 4 personnel.

  3. : Psychological evaluations are to be administered weekly to assess any cognitive changes or signs of distress.

  4. : Any attempt by SCP-007 to conceal or alter the anomaly must be reported immediately and containment protocols reviewed.

Description:

SCP-007 is a male human, approximately 25 years of age, of European descent. Subject is in good health and shows no signs of physical or mental abnormality, except for the presence of a spherical region of space approximately 60 cm in diameter located in the subjectâ€™s abdominal cavity.

This region appears to be a miniature ecosystem, containing soil, grass, and a small central body of water. Weather patterns have been observed within the anomaly, including rainfall and occasional cloud formation. The subject does not appear to experience any discomfort from the presence of this anomaly, nor does it interfere with normal bodily functions.

The origin of SCP-007 is unknown. Subject claims to have been born with the anomaly, though no records exist to verify this. Attempts to probe or analyze the anomaly have been met with resistance from the subject and have yielded inconclusive results.
